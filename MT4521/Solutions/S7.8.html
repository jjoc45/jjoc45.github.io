If you think of the cards as extending out to the faces of a cube, you get the picture on the right.
So (as in Exercises 5 Question 3(c)) the rotational symmetry group is A4 (consisting of three rotations by π about the centres of faces and 8 rotations by ±2π/3 about the diagonals (but no rotations by ±π/2 about the faces or rotations about the centres of edges).
Since the figure has central symmetry, the full rotation group is A4 × < J >.
