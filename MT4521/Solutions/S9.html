  3. Go round the curves starting with a and when you get back to the vertex take the next available path "going round anticlockwise" -- that is, "turn left" when you get back to the vertex. This will give you the boundary of a 12-gon with edge word
    a b c d-1 c-1 d e f-1 e-1 f a-1 b-1

    1. Not a planar model: too many as
    2. Not a planar model of a compact surface: c is a free edge. (It is K # T with a disc removed!)
    3. Is a planar model of an orientable compact surface
    4. Is a planar model of an non-orientable compact surface (b-1 is matched with itself)
    5. Is a planar model of an orientable compact surface
    6. Not a planar model of a compact surface: a is a free edge
    7. Is a planar model of an non-orientable compact surface
