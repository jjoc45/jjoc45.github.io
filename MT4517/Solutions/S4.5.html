Solution 4.5

Invariance of multiplication fails for the first and invariance of addition fails for the third. Note that since C is a field the kernel of any homomorphism is either {0} or C and so these clearly cannot be homomorphisms.
The second is an isomorphism from C to C as you can verify.
The fourth is not a homomorphism since, for example, 1 ↦ i but 12 ↦ 1 ≠ i2 = -1.
