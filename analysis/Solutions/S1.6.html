Suppose √3 = a/b. Then 3b2 = a2 and so a is divisible by 3. Then the RHS is divisible by 9 and so b2 is divisible by 3 and b is divisible by 3. Thus a, b have a common factor and we could have assumed that a/b was in "Lowest terms" and get a contradiction.
